B.E.E.R

Blockchain Enabled Energy Recommender

A mechanism to decentralize the transfer of energy



Currently, there are only a few energy providers and you’re essentially forced to pay whatever price they charge. With our recommendation system, we intend to change that. The steps involved are as follows:

1) Track the energy consumption profiles of each house in the network
2) Forecast the energy required by the house in the next hour for each node, and match the house with excess generation with the one in demand of energy.
3) Facilitate exchange of ether and energy between the matched houses.

With blockchain technology, the energy market of the future can be open, competitive, and two-sided. People will have more choices, be charged based on their individual requirements, and can even sell energy back to the market.
